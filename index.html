About Me

Hello! My name is Sophie, but I am known as Nhi in Vietnamese. My family is from Vietnam, a small country in Southeast Asia, and I came to Calgary when I was 14 years old. The rich culture, landscapes, and warm people of Vietnam have left a lasting impression on me. Currently, I'm a student at the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology (SAIT). My focus is on User Experience in the Interactive Design program. I have been drawn to this field because it combines my love of creativity and technology.
